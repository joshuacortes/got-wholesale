Small Business Owners Can Apply Now for Lift ME Accelerator Program

Mount Pleasant, MI — Central Michigan University has officially opened applications for the Lift ME Accelerator Program, a free startup accelerator designed to support early-stage entrepreneurs and small-business owners across Michigan. The program aims to empower founders with the tools, mentorship, and resources needed to grow their businesses in a competitive market.

A Free Accelerator Built for Michigan Entrepreneurs

The Lift ME Accelerator was created to remove barriers for aspiring business owners who may lack access to capital, formal training, or experienced mentors. Designed by CMU’s Isabella Bank Institute for Entrepreneurship, the program focuses on helping individuals turn promising ideas into sustainable businesses.

This year’s cohort will receive hands-on guidance in several key areas, including:

  • Business planning and goal setting
  • Branding, marketing, and customer acquisition strategies
  • Financial planning and cash-flow management
  • Pitch development and investor readiness
  • Networking opportunities with industry leaders

Program Insight:

The Lift ME Accelerator is completely free — making it one of the few no-cost business development programs available to Michigan entrepreneurs.

Who Should Apply?

The program accepts applicants from all backgrounds, industries, and stages of development. Whether an entrepreneur has a brand-new idea or a small business already in operation, CMU encourages individuals with a strong drive and commitment to growth to apply.

The accelerator is especially beneficial for:

  • First-time founders seeking structured business guidance
  • Small-business owners looking to scale
  • Individuals from underserved communities
  • Owners needing help navigating funding challenges
  • Entrepreneurs without access to traditional startup networks

Why Lift ME Stands Out

Many accelerators charge thousands of dollars or require founders to give up equity in exchange for mentorship. Lift ME takes a different approach — offering its programming entirely free of charge.

The initiative reflects Central Michigan University's mission to uplift local economies and foster innovation across the state. By removing financial barriers, the university helps entrepreneurs focus on building strong, resilient companies.

Did You Know?

Small businesses make up 99.6% of Michigan’s businesses, underscoring the importance of accelerators that provide real-world support without financial burden.

Program Benefits: A Deep Dive

Participants in the Lift ME Accelerator can expect a robust curriculum paired with personalized mentorship. The program includes:

  • Weekly workshops hosted by industry professionals
  • One-on-one coaching with experienced mentors
  • Access to CMU’s entrepreneurial ecosystem and partner organizations
  • Pitch practice sessions to help founders refine their messaging
  • Networking events designed to connect participants with investors, advisors, and peer entrepreneurs

The combination of practical learning and expert guidance allows small-business owners to gain the confidence and clarity needed to navigate the challenges of modern entrepreneurship.

Application Process and Key Dates

Applications are now open and can be completed online through CMU’s entrepreneurship program portal. The university recommends applying early due to limited cohort spots.

Founders should be prepared to share:

  • A short description of their business or idea
  • The problem they aim to solve
  • Current challenges they’re facing
  • Their long-term vision and goals

Voices from the Community

Local entrepreneurs who completed previous CMU business programs have praised the university’s commitment to accessible education. Many say the mentorship and accountability helped them gain traction, secure funding, or pivot their ideas with greater confidence.

Economic leaders in the region believe the Lift ME Accelerator will play a meaningful role in strengthening Michigan’s small-business landscape, especially for founders in rural or underserved areas.

Why Now Is the Perfect Time to Apply

With economic uncertainties, evolving consumer behavior, and rising costs, many small businesses are seeking guidance now more than ever. Programs like Lift ME offer support at a critical moment — helping founders stabilize operations, build resilience, and identify new opportunities for growth.

Bottom Line:

The Lift ME Accelerator offers Michigan entrepreneurs a rare opportunity: free, high-quality business support with no strings attached.

Conclusion

Central Michigan University’s Lift ME Accelerator Program provides an invaluable pathway for small-business owners and new founders ready to elevate their ideas. By offering free education, mentorship, and resources, CMU is empowering Michigan’s next generation of entrepreneurs to build strong, sustainable businesses — and applications are open now.

Entrepreneurs interested in joining the upcoming cohort are encouraged to apply as soon as possible, as space is limited and demand is expected to be high.
