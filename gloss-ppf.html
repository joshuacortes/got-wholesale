Gloss PPF – Onyxhield Series

Optimized Optical Properties

With superior transmittance, excellent reflection control, and amazing refraction capabilities, Onyxhield Gloss PPF reveals the vehicle's true color in any lighting condition. Say goodbye to haze and distortion — enjoy clear, high-definition surface performance.
